Jeff Hancock Featured in New Documentary with Bill Gates

In the new Netflix documentary, What's Next? The Future with Bill Gates, Jeff Hancock speaks to the ethical dilemmas and other challenges of AI.
  • WATCH ON NETFLIX

What's Next? The Future with Bill Gates

 

Join visionary philanthropist Bill Gates as he delves into pressing global issues and uncovers cutting-edge technologies that will transform the world. The Netflix documentary, What's Next? The Future with Bill Gates, features Professor Jeff Hancock offering his insights on AI—its recent developments, inherent biases, and the ethical dilemmas in this ever-evolving space. 

 

Watch the show for crucial discussions around AI's impact on our world with Jeff Hancock, Director of the Stanford Social Media Lab and Stanford Internet Observatory, and Co-Director of the Stanford Cyber Policy Center.
